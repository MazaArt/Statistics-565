        \section*{Project Plan}
        \begin{itemize}
        \item Team: Solo
        \item Goal: Replicate the methods and results from 'Topological Data Analysis of Financial Time Series: Landscapes of Crashes' by Marian Gidea and Yuri Katz, \url{https://arxiv.org/pdf/1703.04385}. 
        \item Why I am interested: Interesting combination of math towards analysis of financial series beyond what we learn in the course contents. Good application of the theory from my math major towards this course's material. 
        \item Why this matters: What if they faked their data... Also an interesting perspective of replicating the research methodologies (which are much more complicated than something that I could come up with). 
        \item Challenges foreseen: The fact that I do not know how to apply Topological Data Analysis (TDA) towards time series \textit{yet...} both Python code-wise and mathematical foundations.
        \item Fix to the challenges: Found the potential resources listed below to learn TDA and its application towards time series through a set of different research papers and a sequence of Medium intro walk-throughs with the code introductions.
        \item Data Source: Intending to use \texttt{Yahoo Finance} Python package to source financial equity market data. 
        \item Methodologies: Implementing the methodology put forward in the original paper, with potential segways/additions by implementing the methodologies proposed in 'Forecasting Financial Crisis using Topological Data Analysis Approach' from African Scientific Annual Review, \url{https://www.researchgate.net/publication/380043077_Forecasting_Financial_Crisis_using_Topological_Data_Analysis_Approach}. 
        \end{itemize}
